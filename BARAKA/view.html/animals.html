ANIMAL HUSBANDRY.

Animal Husbandry Course

Course Overview

This intensive course on animal husbandry provides in-depth knowledge and practical skills for managing and caring for various types of animals. Participants will learn about animal nutrition, breeding, health management, and farm practices.

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Animal Husbandry
  • Animal Nutrition and Feeding
  • Animal Breeding and Genetics
  • Animal Health and Disease Management
  • Farm Management and Practices

Course Duration

The course duration is 12 weeks, consisting of both theoretical lectures and practical hands-on sessions.

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of biology and animal science
  • Prior experience or knowledge in farming or animal care is beneficial

Course Schedule

Week Topic
1 Introduction to Animal Husbandry
2-3 Animal Nutrition and Feeding
4-5 Animal Breeding and Genetics
6-7 Animal Health and Disease Management
8-12 Farm Management and Practices
